Target Audience

A description of the target audience:

The target audience is going to match the same audience as Pokemon Go. This will focus on young adults between the ages of twenty and thirty. A bias will be towards men who have a higher regular log in average than women. There will be some additional focus on locals to the Payson Idaho area.

Two Personas:

Pokemon Go Die hard: this person is someone who is more interested in being up to date on the local Pokemon Go updates. Whether this be a change in gym leadership, a rare pokemon being spotted, or special deals on the pokemon game. They will participate in the events but the main focus is on the actual game.

Pokemon Socializer: this person is someone who uses Pokemon Go to get outside and spend time with other people. They are more interested in going to the events in order to find other people who share similar interest to them. They want to know where people are going to be gathering, and to see if they can find other people who want to talk with them about Pokemon Go.
